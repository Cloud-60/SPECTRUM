Call for Papers


The conference invites submission of papers on the topics given below but not limited to:


  • Quantum Computing: Algorithms, applications, and computational advancements.
  • Artificial Intelligence in Healthcare: AI-driven diagnostics, treatment plans, and health tech innovations.
  • Gene Editing and Biotechnology: CRISPR-Cas9 and its impact on gene therapy and biotechnology.
  • Nanotechnology: Drug delivery systems and future biomedical prospects.
  • Machine Learning for Climate Change: Predictive analytics and sustainable tech
  • Renewable Energy: Solar, wind, and other renewable energy sources.
  • Blockchain and Data Security: Decentralized finance and security innovations.
  • Autonomous Vehicles: Developments, safety, and ethical considerations.
  • 3D Printing in Biomedical Engineering: Customization of prosthetics, implants, and tissue engineering.
  • Energy Storage Solutions: Next-generation battery technologies and advancements.
  • Space Exploration: Space missions, satellite technology, and deep-space innovations.
  • Internet of Things (IoT): Smart devices, integration, and networked environments.
  • Synthetic Biology: Biotechnology innovations in life forms and medicine.
  • Virtual and Augmented Reality: Innovations in human-computer interaction.
  • Big Data Analytics: Business intelligence, data-driven decision making, and analytics.
  • Cybersecurity: Threats, protection methods, and digital security advancements.
  • Robotics and Automation: Manufacturing, automation, and the future of work.
  • Bioprinting: Organ and tissue fabrication technology.
  • Advanced Materials Science: Innovations in superconductors, metamaterials, and smart materials.
  • Neuroscience and AI: Bridging cognitive science with artificialÂ intelligence.


All full length papers submitted will be peer reviewed. Selected full length papers after peer review will be published.
